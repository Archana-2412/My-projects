PM Modi reaches out to farmers, Congress stages walkout in Lok Sabha

Addressing the Lok Sabha on Wednesday, PM Narendra Modi said the Congress is such a divided party that its own MPs in LS and RS cannot agree with each other on issues.Prime Minister Narendra Modi vociferously defended his government's performance in Lok Sabha while concluding the discussion on the Motion of Thanks to the President on Wednesday. The Congress, on the other hand, led by former party president Rahul Gandhi staged a walkout from the Lower House midway of the PM's speech.
